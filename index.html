Katie Zbytnuik

University of Calgary, Bachelors of Science, Kinesiology

Hello, Welcome to my website! My name is Katie Zbytnuik, I am a second year student at the University of Calgary studying a Bachelors of Science in Kinesiology. I am passionate about understanding how the human body works, especially in areas regarding exercsie physiology and health. Through my studies I have developed skills in data analysis, research and health. I am interested in pursuing a future career in the medical field, where I am able to apply my knowledge and help others. This website highlights my work, projects and academic experiences.

Overview

Achievements

Graduated from PVSS in Armstrong, BC, with honours. Was awarded the Karlina Grace Muik Bursary in 2023. Recipient of the District Authority Scholarship, Imperial Order of the Daughters Empire bursary, Armstrong Bottle Depot Bursary. Completed DELF A1.

Future Plans

After completing an undergraduate degree at the University of Calgary with a Bachelors of Science, Kinesiology, I am going to apply for the Physician Assistant graduate program at the University of Calgary. After completing my Graduate studies I plan to work for a few years, and then apply for medicine. /p>

Volunteer

I have been involved in a variety of volunteer opportunites, both from my home community and the Calgary community. In Armstrong, I volunteered at the local dance studio, assiting teachers and helping run summer camps. I have aslo been apart of the First Shift hockey program in both Armstrong, BC and Calgary, AB. First shift is a non-profit orgaization for children to learn the basics of hockey. Additionally I volunteered at Brentwood Physio in Calgary. My role at Brentwood Physio was shadowing physiotherapists, cleaning the clinic, patient care, along with scanning documents and uploading to patient files.

Work

I have had a variety of different jobs and am constantly trying to expand my skills. My first job was at Askews Foods in Armstrong, Bc, I worked here for a year and a half. I assited customers, stocked shelves and familiarized myself with POS systems. Askews was an amzing opportunity for me to expand my social skills and gain customer service. Aditionally, I wokred at Starbucks in Armstrong, BC, this job was a great opprotunity for me to familiarize myself with fast paced work, I was able to maintian my customer service demeanor in high stress, fast paced situations. Working at starbucks also helped me improve organiation and follow instructions carefully. My next job was a sumemr job where i had the opportunity to serve and bartend at the local golf course, The Overlander. Serving added onto my building blocks regarding customer service in a high paced scenario. While working all these jobs I also worked as a general labourer at Okanagan Aggregates. This job was great as I am able to work outside and learn working in a setting that detatches from the customer service that I am so used to. Overall I am very greatful for these opportunites and how much they have allowed me to grow and improve as both a worker and an individual.
