I am passionate about understanding how the human body works, especially in areas regarding exercsie physiology and health. Future Plans

After completing an undergraduate degree at the University of Calgary with a Bachelors of Science, Kinesiology, I am going to apply for the Physician Assistant graduate program at the University of Calgary. After completing my Graduate studies I plan to work for a few years, and then apply for medicine.

Volunteer

I have been involved in a variety of volunteer opportunites, both from my home community and the Calgary community. In Armstrong, I volunteered at the local dance studio, assiting teachers and helping run summer camps. I have aslo been apart of the First Shift hockey program in both Armstrong, BC and Calgary, AB. First shift is a non-profit orgaization for children to learn the basics of hockey. Additionally I volunteered at Brentwood Physio in Calgary. My role at Brentwood Physio was shadowing physiotherapists, cleaning the clinic, patient care, along with scanning documents and uploading to patient files.

Work

My first job was at Askews Foods in Armstrong, Bc, I worked here for a year and a half assisting customers, stocking shelves and familiarizing myself with POS systems. Aditionally, I worked at Starbucks in Armstrong, BC, this job was a great for fast paced work, I was able to maintain my customer service demeanor in high stress, fast paced situations. My next job was a summer job where I had the opportunity to serve and bartend at the local golf course, The Overlander. Serving added onto my building blocks regarding customer service in a fast paced scenario. While working all these jobs I also worked as a general labourer at Okanagan Aggregates.
